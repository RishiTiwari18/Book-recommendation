Fiction Books

The Great Gatsby

By F. Scott Fitzgerald

A classic novel about love, wealth, and the American Dream in the Roaring Twenties.

1984

By George Orwell

A dystopian novel that critiques totalitarianism and the dangers of surveillance.

To Kill a Mockingbird

By Harper Lee

A powerful novel about racism, justice, and the loss of innocence in the American South.

Non-fiction Books

Educated

By Tara Westover

A memoir about a young woman who escapes her survivalist family to pursue an education.

Becoming

By Michelle Obama

The inspiring memoir of the former First Lady, reflecting on her life and experiences.

Sapiens

By Yuval Noah Harari

A compelling history of humankind, tracing the evolution of Homo sapiens from ancient to modern times.

Science Fiction Books

Dune

By Frank Herbert

An epic space opera that explores politics, religion, and ecology on the desert planet of Arrakis.

Neuromancer

By William Gibson

A groundbreaking cyberpunk novel that has become a genre-defining classic.

Foundation

By Isaac Asimov

A science fiction saga about the rise and fall of galactic empires and the search for knowledge.

Fantasy Books

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone

By J.K. Rowling

The start of Harry Potter's magical journey at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

The Lord of the Rings

By J.R.R. Tolkien

An epic fantasy tale of friendship, courage, and the battle against evil to save Middle-earth.

A Game of Thrones

By George R. R. Martin

A dark, thrilling story of power, betrayal, and survival in a medieval-inspired world.
